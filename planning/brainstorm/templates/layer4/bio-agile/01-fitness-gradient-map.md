## Fitness Landscape Concept

**Biological Metaphor:** Species evolve by climbing fitness peaks (better adaptations to environment). The landscape has:
- **Peaks:** High fitness (well-adapted)
- **Valleys:** Low fitness (poorly adapted)
- **Ridges:** Multiple viable paths
- **Plateaus:** Local optima (good enough, but not global best)

**Product Translation:** Product features/strategies have varying fitness in the market environment. Evolution requires navigating this landscape toward higher fitness.
