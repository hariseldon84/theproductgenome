## Key Findings

### 1. Trustworthy Online Controlled Experiments — Kohavi, Tang, Xu
- **Authors**: Ron Kohavi, Diane Tang, Ya Xu
- **Publisher**: Cambridge University Press
- **Subtitle**: "A Practical Guide to A/B Testing"
- **Foundation**: Practical experiences at companies running **20,000+ controlled experiments per year**
- **Author backgrounds**:
  - **Ron Kohavi**: Previously VP/Technical Fellow at Airbnb; prior: Technical Fellow & Corporate VP at Microsoft (led Experimentation Platform team - ExP); previously led Weblab at Amazon
  - **Diane Tang**: Google Fellow — expertise in large-scale data analysis/infrastructure, online controlled experiments, ads systems
  - **Ya Xu**: Heads Data Science and Experimentation at LinkedIn — published papers on experimentation, frequent speaker at conferences/universities
- **Content**: Examples, pitfalls, advice for students and industry professionals
- **Depth**: Deeper dives into advanced topics for practitioners improving data-driven decisions
- **Companion site**: experimentguide.com — additional material, errata, open discussion
- **Charity**: Authors donate all proceeds to charity
